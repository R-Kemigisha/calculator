How to make a Friut salad

Are you feeling in the mood of a fruit salad
then let's dive in Mhmmm...

Let's start with the "how" process:

Step 1

We should set aside all the fruits
so that they can be washed thoroughly.

Step 2

Cut the fruits in any shape that you desire.

Step 3

Put all the fruits in a bowl of your choice and shape.
For example a rectangle or oval shaped can be perfect.

Step 4

For the liquid Ingredients

    400g Condensed milk 400g Cream cheese Egg yolk 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla essence
Whisk to your ideal texture.

Step 5

Pour the liquid mixture into the fruits.

Step 6

Refrigerate the Fruit salad for 2 hours and you add
any toppings of your choice.
